# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in Tenvra a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of
age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level
of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion,
or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful, precise, and constructive.
- Critique ideas and evidence, not people.
- Disclose conflicts of interest.
- Respect privacy and confidential security reports.
- Do not manipulate reputation, benchmarks, contributor rewards, or governance processes.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized conduct, doxxing, spam, deliberate misinformation,
security exploitation outside the disclosure policy, and attempts to falsify work or identity are
not tolerated.

## Enforcement

Reports are reviewed confidentially by project maintainers. Actions may include a warning, content
removal, temporary suspension, permanent removal, node quarantine, or referral to relevant
authorities. Significant decisions include a reason and an appeal path where safety permits.

## Reporting

Community conduct reports should use the private contact listed in the official project website.
Security vulnerabilities must follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

This policy is based on Contributor Covenant 2.1:
https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/
