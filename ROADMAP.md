# Roadmap

## Phase 0 - Foundation

- Repository scaffold, governance, security policy, contracts, and CI. **Implemented.**
- European-first sovereignty decision and operating principles. **Documented.**
- Company formation, domain acquisition, and trademark clearance.
- Public website and verified interest form. **Implemented locally; production services and legal
  launch gate remain.**

## Phase 1 - Validation

- Protected operator qualification pipeline. **Implemented locally.**
- 100 verified sign-ups.
- 30 GPU owners, including 15 pilot-compatible systems.
- 20 qualified developers or researchers.
- 10 design-partner interviews and 5 non-binding letters of intent.
- Validate whether EU/EEA residency and model independence materially affect purchasing decisions.

## Phase 2 - Research Pilot

- Go compute client and signed job protocol.
- NVIDIA/CUDA and AMD/ROCm allowlists.
- Ten EU/EEA external nodes, including at least three AMD nodes.
- Seventy-two hours of continuous operation.
- At least 95% verified and reproducible accepted work.
- Reproducible candidate-model pipeline with explicit release gates and rollback.

## Phase 3 - Compliance And Security

- Dataset and model documentation.
- GPAI legal assessment.
- EU/EEA data-residency and subprocessor review.
- Critical-provider exit plans and a successful restore into a second approved environment.
- Demonstrated model serving without a third-party hosted model API.
- Independent code review and penetration test.
- Tax, payout, insurance, privacy, and contract review.

## Phase 4 - Paid Design-Partner Beta

- Ten verified European B2B teams.
- Trusted EU/EEA inference infrastructure.
- Usage billing and auditable contributor ledger.
- Public status, incident, and economic reporting.

Dates remain evidence-driven. The planning baseline is 12-15 months to a paid beta after work
begins, subject to financing and technical validation.
