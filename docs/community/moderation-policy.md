# Community Moderation Policy

The community uses the repository Code of Conduct as its baseline.

## Moderation Actions

Actions may include content removal, warning, temporary restriction, node quarantine, account
suspension, or permanent removal. Economic holds and trust penalties require separate evidence and
must not be used merely to resolve interpersonal disagreement.

## Process

- Reports are handled privately.
- Conflicts of interest require a different reviewer.
- Material decisions include a reason and retained audit record.
- Appeals are reviewed by someone other than the original decision-maker where practical.
- Emergency safety actions may precede full review.
