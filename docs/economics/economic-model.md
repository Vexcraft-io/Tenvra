# Economic Model

## Revenue

Planned revenue sources:

- Prepaid API usage.
- Approved postpaid B2B usage with credit limits.
- Team platform subscriptions.
- Later enterprise support or contracted trusted infrastructure.

## Contributor Pool

For the first twelve monetized months, at least 40% of distributable revenue is allocated to
verified contributors.

Distributable revenue excludes:

- VAT and equivalent taxes.
- Refunds and chargebacks before settlement.
- Payment processing fees.
- Direct external overflow compute used to deliver the billed service.

Unallocated contributor funds remain in a separately reported community reserve.

## Rewards

- Work is priced by verified useful units, difficulty, quality, and service constraints.
- Hardware brand, social level, and wealth do not change the value of identical verified work.
- A guaranteed minimum is shown only after the amount is reserved from prepaid funds, an approved
  training budget, or company reserve.
- Settlement waits 30 days before funds become payable.
- Initial payouts target verified Swedish businesses and sole traders.
- The initial payout threshold is the equivalent of SEK 500.

## Company Sustainability

- Target gross margin after direct delivery costs: 25-35%.
- Build six months of fixed-cost reserve before profit distributions.
- Reinvest at least half of early distributable surplus.
- Planning break-even: approximately SEK 670,000 monthly revenue at a 30% contribution margin and
  SEK 200,000 fixed monthly cost.

This is a planning model, not a promise of earnings to contributors or investors.
