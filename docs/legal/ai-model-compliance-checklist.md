# AI And Model Compliance Checklist

> Planning checklist only. Qualified legal review is required before a model or paid service is
> released.

Before model release:

- Base-model license and downstream obligations reviewed.
- General-purpose AI classification assessed.
- Technical documentation completed.
- Copyright compliance policy approved.
- Training-content summary prepared.
- Dataset provenance and removal process tested.
- Model card, limitations, and intended use published.
- Security and memorization evaluation completed.
- Incident and serious-risk reporting responsibilities assigned.
- Open-source claims checked against the actual license terms.

Open development and community compute do not transfer provider responsibility away from Tenvra.
