# Contributor Terms Outline

> Outline for counsel, not enforceable terms.

Future terms should cover:

- Independent service-provider status for paid node operators.
- Participant control over schedule, jobs, price floor, and equipment.
- No employment, profit, reward, or future-payment guarantee.
- Rights to contributed code, data, adapters, evaluations, and training outputs.
- Hardware, electricity, tax, and ordinary wear responsibility.
- Acceptable use and security duties.
- Verification, rejection, quarantine, and appeal process.
- Settlement timing, payout threshold, refunds, fraud, and account closure.
- Confidentiality boundaries and prohibition on private customer inference.
- Governing law and dispute process.
