# Validation Plan

## Thirty-Day Gate

Success requires all of the following:

- 100 verified qualified registrations.
- 30 GPU owners.
- 15 pilot-compatible GPU systems.
- 20 qualified developers or researchers.
- 10 design-partner interviews.
- 5 non-binding letters of intent for a paid B2B beta.

Registrations count only after email verification. Pilot compatibility is assessed against the
current hardware, operating-system, availability, and network allowlist.

## Decision Rule

- At or above all thresholds: proceed to financing and pilot recruitment.
- At least 60% of core thresholds: conduct interviews, revise positioning, and run one final
  30-day cycle.
- Below 60%, or a failed second cycle: pause, narrow, or pivot the project.

## Evidence

Store source attribution, consent, verification time, qualification status, and the reviewer who
made the decision. Do not publish individual hardware ownership or personal data.
